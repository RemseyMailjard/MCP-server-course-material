# How do I check mortgage rates?

## Example
Want to know what interest rate Rabobank offers for your situation?

### Prompt

```
What's the current interest rate at Rabobank for a 10-year fixed mortgage
with the best protection option?
```

### What you get back
You'll see the interest rate you can expect.

---

## More examples

### Different contract lengths
```
What are the Rabobank rates for 5, 10, or 20 years fixed?
```

### Depending on your savings
```
What interest rates are available for a 15-year fixed Rabobank mortgage,
depending on how much I can save?
```

### Your situation
```
I want to buy a house for €300,000 and I have €60,000 saved.
What rate would Rabobank offer for 10 years fixed?
```
