# How much will I pay each month?

## Example
Want to know your monthly payment?

### Prompt

```
How much would I pay each month if I borrow €300,000 from Rabobank,
get a 4.33% interest rate, and pay it back over 30 years?
```

### What you get back
You'll see exactly how much to pay each month.

---

## More examples

### Different payback schedule
```
How much would I pay each month if I pay everything back in 20 years
instead of 30 years? (€300,000 at 4.33%)
```

### Different house prices
```
What would my monthly payments be for:
- €250,000 house (30 years, 4.33%)
- €300,000 house (30 years, 4.33%)
- €350,000 house (30 years, 4.33%)

So I can see the difference.
```

### My budget
```
I have €60,000 saved. How much do I pay per month if I buy a
€300,000 house and pay it back over 30 years at 4.33%?
```
