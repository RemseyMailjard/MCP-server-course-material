# More complex examples

## Example 1: Compare two buying options

### Prompt

```
I want to buy a house for €400,000. I'm considering two options:

Option A: With protection (cheaper rate, but extra costs)
- I save €40,000 (10%)
- 10 years fixed
- Pay back over 30 years

Option B: Without protection (normal rate, more savings needed)
- I save €100,000 (25%)
- 10 years fixed
- Pay back over 30 years

What's my monthly payment for each and which is better for me?
```

---

## Example 2: Fixed short-term or long-term?

### Prompt

```
I'm worried about rising interest rates. What's better for me?

Option 1: 5 years fixed
- After 5 years I can renegotiate

Option 2: 10 years fixed
- More security, not too expensive

Option 3: 20 years fixed
- Completely stable, interest never changes

What would I pay each month for €300,000 in each scenario (30 years total)?
```

---

## Example 3: Should I switch my old mortgage?

### Prompt

```
I took out a mortgage 5 years ago. My old rate was 3.5%,
I still owe €280,000 and the rate has now risen to 4.73%.

Would it be worth switching to Rabobank?
- What do I pay now per month? (25 years left)
- What would I pay at Rabobank? (25 years)
- Does this save me money or cost me more?
```

---

## Example 4: Which house fits my budget?

### Prompt

```
I'm looking at three houses and I can save 25% of the price.
All for 10 years fixed at Rabobank.

House 1: €250,000
House 2: €300,000
House 3: €350,000

How much would I pay each month for each house (30 years total)?
Which house fits my budget best?
```
