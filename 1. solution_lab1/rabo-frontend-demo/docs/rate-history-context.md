# Information about Rabobank interest rates

## What does Rabobank offer?

This tool gives you the current Rabobank interest rates from July 2025.

### How long do you want to lock in your rate?
- **Short**: 1-10 years fixed
- **Medium**: 11-15 years fixed
- **Long**: 20, 25, or 30 years fixed

### How does your savings affect your rate?

| Scenario | What it means | Example |
|----------|--------|----------|
| **With protection** | Lowest rate, but extra insurance costs | You save about 10% |
| **30% or more** | Good rate | €60,000+ on a €200,000 house |
| **10-30%** | Normal rate | €20,000-€60,000 |
| **Less than 10%** | A bit more expensive | Less than €20,000 |
